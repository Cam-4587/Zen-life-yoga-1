About us


We believe that yoga has the power to transform and flush through the mind, body, and soul. In the very heart of the community, at a place that is accessible to people of all ages and abilities, Serenity Flow Yoga Studio opens the door to start down the path of holistic wellness.


Our mission is that, through the practice of yoga, we provide space for the attainment of balance, strength, and inner peace. We are committed to offering you direction and support in looking for discovery and personal growth.


Yoga is more than an exercise; it is an integrated way of well-being that covers mind, body, and spirit.


It has a serene studio space; leave the hustle and bustle of life at the door. With warm, natural light and ambience second to none, this studio is the ideal sanctuary for the reconnection to find your centre.
